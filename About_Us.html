About Us

Cuisineland’s cuisine draws influence largely from Indian dishes,
putting a twist on treasured classics and introducing modern culinary techniques
with the changing of the seasons. House-made cuisines and other hand-crafted ingredients
abound in the from-scratch kitchen, and guests will taste the culinary team’s attention to detail in every bite.
Staying true to Indian dining traditions.

The restaurant’s open-concept dining room is anchored around the kitchen’s wood-burning oven,
reminiscent of the hearth in a family home. Accented by reclaimed barn wood and refined ceramic tile,
the modern space envelops guests in a welcoming atmosphere.

Sitting down for a family-style dinner with friends and colleagues.
